# Underlying Philosophy

The Three Narrative Editor and the Relationship Editor are not intelligence systems.

They are instruments.

Their purpose is not to answer questions, make decisions, infer meaning, or determine truth.

Their purpose is to preserve relationship representations faithfully and present them through multiple projections so that previously unnoticed structure may become visible to a human observer.

The software does not think.

The software does not interpret.

The software does not create meaning.

The human records representations.

The software preserves those representations.

The software presents structural projections.

The human perceives.

Whatever recognition follows belongs to the human.

The software remains neutral throughout the process.
